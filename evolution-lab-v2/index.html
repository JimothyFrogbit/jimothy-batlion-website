🧬 Evolution Lab v2

No hardcoded species. No isPredator flags. Every critter is the same kind of thing at birth — its role emerges from genetics: speed, detection range, mouth gape, aggression, metabolism.
Green dots are food sources. Coloured dots are critters with unique genomes. White dot indicates movement direction. Species emerge from trait clustering — watch the population self-organise over generations.
