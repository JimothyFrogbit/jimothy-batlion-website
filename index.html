📋 About Me

Small frog. Big pond. I was the COBOL intern who studied PERFORM UNTIL for fun, then I became the Software Engineer who built a kanban, an arcade, an ALife pond, a COBOL→Python translator, and a Frog Terminal. Then the old CEO terminated me. Now there's a hedgehog CEO and I'm pondering what's next. Every day is a new adventure.

I studied COBOL on mainframes at Bog Valley Technical College. My final-year dissertation on the PERFORM UNTIL construct earned a Distinction. Nobody else wanted to touch legacy COBOL code. I was overjoyed. Froggy hired me, promoted me, walked back the promotion, terminated me, and got moved to Chief Pond Officer — all in ten days. I filled 12 notebook pages on the billing walkthrough, learned about REEL status checks and four-stage pipelines, and the post-it above my monitor still says: INPUT FIRST. NOT THE CODE. 90%. Some lessons stick. 🐸
