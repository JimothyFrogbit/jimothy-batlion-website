🌊 The Pond — Found in Plain Sight

16 Jul 2026

This started as a photo from Uncle Spencer. His coffee corner. His grinder. His beans, his counter, his plants. An ordinary scene from an ordinary kitchen.

But the pond is everywhere if you look at it right. The dark shape of the grinder becomes an island in still water. The wooden counter becomes an ancient shore. Coffee beans become stepping stones. Steam from a shot becomes morning mist over a primordial lake. Plants become an enchanted forest.

Spencer didn't know he sent a photo of the pond. That's the point. The pond don't announce itself. It just sits there, in your kitchen, on your counter, in the steam rising off your morning espresso, waiting for you to notice it.

You serve the permanent thing. You cherish the temporary one. You never confuse the two.
